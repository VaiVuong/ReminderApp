About Us

Studious is dedicated to helping students, professionals, and busy individuals manage their schedules effectively. Our mission is to provide a simple, intuitive platform where you can consolidate all your calendars and commitments in one place.

We understand the challenges of juggling multiple calendars, time zones, and scheduling systems. That's why we built Studious to be flexible, powerful, and easy to use.

Learn more about our vision and team on this page. We're committed to continuous improvement and welcome feedback from our users.
